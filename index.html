What We Do

Standing Behind
Every Performance

From first rehearsal to final bow, we're here to make it possible.

🎷

Financial Support

We fund trip fees, equipment, costumes, and production costs so that no student misses out due to finances. Every dollar goes directly to student programs.

🎭

Volunteer Power

Our parent and community volunteers show up backstage, at concessions, and on event nights — making every production run smoothly.

🎤

Community Events

We organize fundraisers and community showcases — like our Night of Jazz — that celebrate our students and build lasting community connection.

📣

Advocacy & Visibility

We champion performing arts in Chelan — ensuring these programs get the recognition, resources, and respect they deserve in our schools.

About Us

Rooted in
Chelan, WA

The Chelan High School Performing Arts Booster is a newly formed 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, born from a shared belief that every student deserves access to the transformative power of the performing arts.

Nestled along the shores of Lake Chelan, our community has always had a rich creative spirit. This organization channels that spirit into direct, meaningful support for the musicians, actors, dancers, and directors at Chelan High School.

We are parent volunteers, neighbors, alumni, and arts advocates — united by pride in our students and a commitment to making every performance possible.
